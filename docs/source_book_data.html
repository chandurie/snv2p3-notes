Source Book Data

Source book : Along with Mittheilungen, the source books of Wolf were found in loose, unbound form in 2015. Before his discoveries were printed in the Mittheilungen, Wolf first documentd them in these source tables. Source books offer a lot of supplemetary material.

  1. Observers on particular days, their k-factors and sunspot_observations
  2. Details of observers data used to fill out gaps on Wolf’s data.
  3. Contain additional observers before 1849, which are not published in Mittheilungen.
  4. Contain only 2 instances of Wolfer’s observations
  5. Source books revealed that Wolf interpolated the records to fill in gaps in the observations hed could not make.
